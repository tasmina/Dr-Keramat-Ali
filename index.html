Welcome





Welcome to the page of Professor Shah Mohammad Keramat Ali, Professor of Clinical Nutrition in the Institute of Nutrition and Food Science at University of Dhaka, Bangladesh. He was born on June 24th, 1942. He got his MBBS in 1968 , D.P.H. in 1976 and obtained M CommH from University of Liverpool, England,with Commonwealth Scholarship and got his Ph.D in 1996 from Jahangirnagar University. Alongside being a professor of clinical nutrition, at INFS University of Dhaka, he is trained and practices in educational methodology, primary health care, bio-statistics biomedical research, maternal nutrition, and arsenic epidemiology. Currently he is professor of Public Health in Daffodil International University, Shukrabad, Dhaka. He currently resides in South Bashaboo, Dhaka, Bangladesh.
